About Me

I am an engineer, apart from my regular work, I would love to spend sometime creating new things, exploring places. I always collect/preserve old stuff and makes new usable/creative decor items. Spending time on what I love, gives me immense satisfaction. The idea of having/creating my own page on instagram happened during pandemic. Especially during the covid-19 being involved in these activities has helped in keeping the mental stability. Many of my friends and few strangers have reached out to me saying they really like all of my work and it motivates them as well to create some useful items from unnecessary pieces. My work mostly includes painting(on pots, reusing the cake trays, CDs, notepad hardcover, bookmarks and many more). This has always been an advantage for me as aa part of extra curricular activity at every phase of my life such as during undergraduation, at my work place and even during my graduation. Won many awards in multiple competitions held at college. The support I have received from my friends and family also added to achieve this. I would suggest every single person out there to find their own "CheerFactor" and do something that helps them to be happy. Find your own way of being happy and support your friends/family during their hardtimes.
